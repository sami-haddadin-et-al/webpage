## The Munich Institute of Robotics and Machine Intelligence (MIRMI)

The Munich Institute of Robotics and Machine Intelligence (MIRMI) is an Integrative Research Institute at the Technical University of Munich (TUM). MIRMI is dedicated to driving the development of embodied intelligence by uniting hardware, software, and human-centered design to create trustworthy, sustainable, and sovereign technologies.
