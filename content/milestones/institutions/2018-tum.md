## The Munich Institute of Robotics and Machine Intelligence (MIRMI)

The Munich Institute of Robotics and Machine Intelligence [(MIRMI)](https://www.mirmi.tum.de/en/mirmi/home/) is an Integrative Research Institute at the Technical University of Munich [(TUM)](https://www.tum.de/). MIRMI is dedicated to driving the development of embodied intelligence by uniting hardware, software, and human-centered design to create trustworthy, sustainable, and sovereign technologies.

Sami Haddadin was with the [Chair of Robotics and Systems Intelligence](https://www.ce.cit.tum.de/en/rsi/home/), TUM School of Computation, Information and Technology.
