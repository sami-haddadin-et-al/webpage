## About
Hi, I am David Braun. I lead research and innovation initiatives in visionary robotics technologies.

I have always been fascinated by one idea: creating technologies that move with us and expand what people can do. 
This fascination has guided my work in robotics, where dynamics, optimization, and adaptive design come together to build machines that learn, adjust, and support us in the real world. 
My focus is not only on advancing what is technically possible, but also on shaping technologies that feel natural and empowering in everyday life.
