## About
Dedicated innovator and robotics expert specializing in humanoid robot development, with a passion for leveraging bionic principles to enhance strength and adaptability. 
Currently serving as a six-year group leader for Actuators in the mechatronic department, I contribute to cutting-edge projects. 
With a strong commitment to innovation, my extensive patent portfolio, comprising 18 filed patents with 11 granted, underscores my dedication to advancing robotics.

In addition to my leadership role, I play a pivotal role as a reviewer for the MMX space rover and serve as a steering committee member for the Space Dream Arm project. 
Actively engaging in a DFG soft robotics initiative, I contribute to open-source testbeds, providing valuable resources for cooperation partners.

My vision is centered on relieving humans from dangerous tasks, particularly in firefighting scenarios. 
By applying a bionic approach to robotic systems, I aim to transform humanoid robotics, making significant strides towards real-world applications. 
Join me on the journey to revolutionize robotics and create safer environments for everyone.
