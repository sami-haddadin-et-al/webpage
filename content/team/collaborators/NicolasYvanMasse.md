## About
I develop machine learning algorithms to uncover the underlying mechanisms of Alzheimer's disease and other neuropsychiatric disorders. 
Previously, I developed machine learning models to translate EMG muscle activity into digital commands (Meta Reality Labs), created generative models for small molecule design (Kebotix), and performed research at the intersection between neuroscience and AI (UChicago and Caltech).
