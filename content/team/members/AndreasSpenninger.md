As Head of Industrialization and Safety Manager at Agile Robots SE, I drive the development and large-scale production of industrial-grade robotic products, ensuring the highest standards of functional safety, quality, and reliability. 
With over a decade of leadership experience in industrial automation, I manage multidisciplinary teams and complex projects across the full product lifecycle, from prototyping to production scaling.

My expertise spans robotics, robot control, mechatronics, systems engineering, and both functional and product safety. 
I am TÜV SÜD-certified in Functional Safety, Industrial Cybersecurity (OT), and Quality Management and hold additional credentials including Certified Business Analysis Professional (CBAP), Certified Systems Engineering Professional (CSEP), Certified Associate in Project Management (CAPM), and Six Sigma Black Belt. 
I am the inventor of multiple patents in robotics, physical AI, mechatronics, and control engineering.

Passionate about innovation and excellence, I am committed to advancing state-of-the-art robotic, automation and physical AI technologies that create real-world impact.
