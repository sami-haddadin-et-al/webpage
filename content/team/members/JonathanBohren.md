## About
Jonathan currently works at Amazon, building systems that help Humans help robots help Hmans. 
Previously, Jonathan lead Honeybee Robotics’ Autonomous Systems Group in Brooklyn, New York. 
Jonathan received his PhD in Mechanical Engieering from The Johns Hopkins University as a member of the Laboratory for Computational Sensing and Robotics.
Jonathan attended The University of Pennsylvania from which he received a BSE in Mechanical Engineering, and an MSE in Robotics.
